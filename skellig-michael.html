--- layout: post title: Skellig Michael ---

We took advantage of a long stretch of uncommonly good weather to drive down to Kerry and take the boat over to Skellig Michael. For many hundreds of years, it was the Christian church's western-most and remotest outpost. Now, its a massive puffin colony. Its still a remarkably remote spot - despite what seemed like at least a week of good weather - we were told that our day was the first time the boats had gone out.
